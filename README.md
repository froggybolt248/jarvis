# Jarvis

**A local-first personal AI assistant that actually remembers you.**

Jarvis is an open-source desktop app that acts as your calendar manager, diet coach, gym trainer, study tutor, and personal knowledge bank — all running entirely on your machine. No cloud accounts, no subscriptions, no telemetry.

> ⚠️ Under active development — not yet released.

## Why Jarvis

- **It remembers.** A persistent, human-readable memory (plain markdown, Obsidian-compatible) means you never re-explain yourself.
- **Local-first.** LLM inference (Ollama), speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and search all run on-device. Your life data never leaves your machine.
- **Proactive, not needy.** Morning briefings, "you're about to miss this" alerts, and batched nudges that respect quiet hours — plus a Quiet Feed audit log of everything Jarvis did, so nothing ever happens silently.
- **One assistant, whole life.** Google Calendar, meals and macros, workout programs with progressive overload, spaced-repetition study cards, and chat over your own notes with citations.
